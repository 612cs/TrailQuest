## 一、英文摘要（Abstract）

**章节**：Abstract

**原文内容**：
> Hiking and other outdoor sports are becoming increasingly popular, attracting many new users. These beginners no longer satisfy themselves with simple route information; they place more emphasis on whether the route is suitable for their level, whether the weather is appropriate, and whether they can enjoy unique landscapes. However, there are many problems with the map navigation tools on the market, mainly manifested in incomplete track data, insufficiently timely weather information updates, and recommendation functions that are mostly sorted based on popularity. This article designs and implements an intelligent route recommendation system for outdoor hiking scenarios to address these issues. The system uses a front-end and back-end separation architecture, with the front end using Vue 3 and TypeScript, and the back end utilizing Spring Boot 3, MySQL, and Redis for business processing and interface services. The system integrates DeepSeek, a large language model, in the intelligent service, allowing users to express their travel needs in natural language, and the system matches and recommends routes accordingly.This article uses a random forest model to predict the probability of the appearance of unique landscapes such as sea of clouds, hoar frost, ice formations, and sunrise and sunset, thereby providing environmental assistance information for travel decisions. The platform has achieved the design goals in its main functions such as route search, intelligent dialogue recommendation, weather query, and landscape prediction, which can significantly reduce the cost of information collection for users before their trips.

**降AIGC后的内容**：
> Hiking and other outdoor activities have grown steadily in popularity, drawing in large numbers of newcomers. For these beginners, basic route descriptions are no longer enough — they care more about whether a trail matches their fitness level, whether the weather will hold up, and whether the route offers noteworthy scenery. Yet the mapping and navigation tools currently available fall short in several ways: trail data tends to be patchy, weather updates lag behind real conditions, and most recommendation features simply rank routes by popularity. To tackle these gaps, this paper presents an intelligent route recommendation platform built specifically for outdoor hiking. The system follows a front-end/back-end separated design — Vue 3 with TypeScript on the front end, and Spring Boot 3, MySQL, and Redis handling business logic and API services on the back end. For intelligent features, the platform connects to the DeepSeek large language model, letting users describe what they want in plain language while the system interprets those needs and suggests matching routes. A random forest model is also employed to estimate the likelihood of scenic phenomena such as sea of clouds, rime ice, icicles, and sunrise or sunset, giving users environmental context for their trip planning. Core functions — route search, conversational recommendation, weather lookup, and landscape prediction — all meet the intended design goals, and together they cut down substantially on the effort users spend gathering pre-trip information.

5. **替换泛化动词**：将"进行"、"实现"、"完成"等高度泛化的动词替换为更具体的动作描述。
